About Us – Crewtor

Crewtor is not just a startup — it’s a movement sparked in 2025 by six dreamers who once walked the same uncertain, hope-filled path every JEE aspirant treads. Fueled by experience and empathy, Sauhard Rai, Sunny Parihar (NIT Bhopal), Mahesh Meena (IIT Delhi), Aman Rathore (IIT Kharagpur), Abhinay Patidar (IIT Roorkee), and Shubham Sharma (NIT Trichy) joined forces to reimagine mentorship — not as a service, but as a mission to guide the next generation with heart, hustle, and honesty.

We’ve faced the pressure, the sleepless nights, the endless doubts — and we wished there was someone who truly understood what we were going through.

That’s why we built Crewtor — to be the kind of mentors we once needed.

At Crewtor, every student becomes part of a “Crew”, guided by a real IITian mentor — whom we proudly call a Captain. Our Captains don’t teach subjects. Instead, they offer something far more powerful — personal guidance, smart study planning, motivation, mindset coaching, and the kind of support that only someone who’s been in your shoes can provide.

🚀 Why Crewtor?

  • Be part of a Crew, not just a platform
  • Mentorship from real IITians — not just tutors
  • Focus on strategy, mindset & consistency over textbooks
  • Tailored for GenZ JEE warriors
  • 100% aligned with the JEE preparation journey

🎯 Our Vision

To empower and guide as many JEE aspirants as possible — making their preparation smarter, stronger, and more emotionally resilient. We’re not just mentors; we’re teammates in your journey, because we’ve played the same game — and we know how to win it.

💬 What We Believe

We get the JEE grind — because we’ve lived it. And that’s why we believe the right mentor can change the way you prepare, perform, and push forward.
